Events

Fall 2015 - Spring 2016

  • Sept 9

    First General Body Meeting

    Our first GBM of the year will take place on 9/9 at 7pm in White Hall 110. Come to learn more about GCC and open positions on the executive board! Free pizza and drinks will be provided.

  • Oct 30

    Chinese Desserts 101

    Come learn how to make Chinese pumpkin cake, and get a taste of Halloween! Drinks and assorted Chinese desserts will also be provided. This 'class' will be held in the Fevans Multi-purpose Room from 6:00pm to 9:00pm.

    • Nov 6

      Culture Shock

      Don't miss Emory's third annual Culture Shock, where Emory GCC will join numerous campus culture clubs to hold a fun filled celebration with performances, music, and free food! Culture Shock will take place on Friday, 11/6 from 5:30pm-9:30pm at the Woodruff Health Sciences Center.

    • Nov 23

      Social Reform in China Panel

      On 11/23, Emory GCC will host a speaker event with Professors Thomas Remington and Ning Yu to discuss social reform in China. The event will be held at 7pm in White Hall 100. Food and drinks will be provided.

    • March/April

      China Summit

      Join us for our fourth annual China Summit. Teams will compete in a case competition to solve China's pressing issues and win cash prizes!
