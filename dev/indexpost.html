Mission

Emory Global China Connection is one of over 60 chapters of GCC Global, an international non-profit organization that strives to help Chinese and non-Chinese college students build connections, and assist them in developing the skills and long-term positive relationships necessary to succeed both in China and internationally.

Founded in 2011, GCC Emory is the fastest growing Chinese-interest club on campus. We engage our members by holding meaningful monthly events, pairing Chinese incoming students with American mentors, and connecting members to relevant research, internship and volunteering opportunities. One of our biggest events is China Summit, which is held in March every year.



Promote Global Friendship

Creating opportunities for Chinese and non-Chinese students to socialize, learn, and work together.

Raise Awareness of China

Advancing public understanding of China through meaningful events.

Host China Summit

The largest case competition on China in the Southeastern United States.
