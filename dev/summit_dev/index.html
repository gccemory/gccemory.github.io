About China Summit



Emory Global China Connection is hosting the 4th annual China Summit of the Southeast region in April 2016. This year we are continuing the case competition format. The Summit involves students from diverse backgrounds coming together to dissect and solve present-day issues in China. Students will further their knowledge on China, develop critical thinking skills, and polish research and presentation skills. Speakers and judges will learn about the younger generation's perspectives of China and their creative approach to problem-solving.

Students will be invited to develop and present solutions to China's modern-day challenges, role playing as teams of consultants hired by the government. Topics will relate to different economic objectives that the Chinese government is trying to achieve. Teams will have the opportunity to present their solutions to a panel of professional judges at China Summit. Teams will be judged based on their creativity and practicality.
